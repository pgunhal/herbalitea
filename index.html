Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to pay to use the custom AI chat?

The app is completely free to use; this includes the custom AI for discovering tailored recipes.

Can I save recipes for later use?

Yes, you can save recipes from both the AI and our custom library of teas to a personal database.

Can I buy the tea ingredients from the app?

While we do not sell any products directly, our app does allow you to purchase tea and ingredients through redirecting you to the appropriate sites.
