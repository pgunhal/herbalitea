Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log my tea consumption?

You can log your tea consumption by clicking on the 'Log Tea' button on the main screen of the app.

Can I set reminders for tea time?

Yes, you can set reminders by navigating to the 'Reminders' section in the app settings.
